What is C3?

The Content Creator Carnival is a creative carnival where we challenge you to be your best. Over 5-6 days, we provide teens and pre-teens with a highly stimulating, state-of-the-art environment to experiment with professional equipment and software.

Why C3?

This is not babysitting. This is transformation.

C3 is not a babysitting camp but a transformative experience geared towards empowering teens and improving their confidence and creativity.

At C3, we center a child's confidence and cognitive development and use theatre, film, music, and song to help them improve their language, social, and leadership skills.

We do not see ourselves as a camp but a once in a lifetime transformative experience that helps them become the person the world isn't allowing them to be.
